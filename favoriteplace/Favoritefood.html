CHAMPORADO

Champorado or tsampurado is a sweet chocolate rice porridge made with sticky rice and tablea. Although it traces its roots back from the Mexican champurrado, the Filipino version has adapted to Chinese influences. CHICKEN CURRY

Pinoy Chicken Curry recipe is the Filipino version of the popular chicken curry dish. It is delicious and easy to cook. The sauce is rich, creamy, and flavorful. It goes well with warm white rice.
